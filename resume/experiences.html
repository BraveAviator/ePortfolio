NASA STEM Educator - Cosmosphere, Hutchinson KS (May 2024 - July 2024)

Roughly 40-50 hours a week, I managed up to 30 young campers at summer space camp, which included overnight accommodations. I learned and implemented necessary technical skills to operate equipment that were used for activities, including various robots, JavaScript coding, 3D printing and modeling, breadboard circuitry, rocketry and much more. I also had the opportunity to collaborate with astronauts Don Thomas and John Herrington in which we conducted camp activities. This aligns with my long-term goals because the topic I educate these young campers on are topics in which I plan on working on in the future. Along with that, being able to lead young campers translates to leading future co-workers in team projects as the fundamental leadership skills and concepts are still the same.
