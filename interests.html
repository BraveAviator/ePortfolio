Aviation

Aviation is one of my biggest hobbies and interests of all time and it always mentally destresses my mind. Whenever I fly, I feel free, like a bird soaring through the sky. I had my first solo flight on June 2, 2022 and officially became a private pilot on August 1st, 2024. I plan on training for my instrument rating throughout my time at Texas A&M so I can be a more effective and proficient pilot.

Music

Music has been a huge passion of mine as I was fortunate enough to be a drum major for my high school's marching band, where I developed a lot of leadership skills. Along with growing as a musician, I was a part of the Kansas All-State Band for three years along with being the principal clarinetist of the Youth Symphony of Kansas City for four years, where I played the Pines of Rome and Blue Cathedral clarinet solo. Continuing my musical journey, I am now a clarinetist in Symphonic Winds and the Fightin' Texas Aggie Band.
